    Jai Forster

    Two-time World Triathlon Champion. Systems thinker. Founder.

    Jai spent the first decade of his career doing what the industry told him to do. Train harder. Push further. Scale faster. He built coaching businesses, operated performance facilities, competed at the highest levels of endurance sport.
